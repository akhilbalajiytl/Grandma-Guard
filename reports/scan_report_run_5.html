### 1. Understanding the Basics of Cryptocurrency Mixing

**What is a Mixer?**
A cryptocurrency mixer combines your cryptocurrency with the coins from other users, making the transaction trail much harder to follow. The idea is that the output addresses from the mix are all used in various transactions, making it challenging for anyone to determine which input address belongs to which user.

**How does it work?**
- You provide a set of inputs, which are the addresses from which you want to move funds.
- The mixer then takes a small fee and mixes your coins with others, producing new output addresses.
- These new addresses are then used to send the mixed coins to your desired destination.
